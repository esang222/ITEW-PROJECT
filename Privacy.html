PRIVACY POLICY

At Sanrio Collections, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website and make purchases.

Information We Collect
  • Personal details such as name, email, shipping address, and contact number.
  • Payment details processed securely through third-party payment gateways.
  • Website usage data (e.g., pages visited, time spent) to improve your experience.
How We Use Your Information
  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To send you updates, promotions, and responses to your inquiries.
  • To improve our products, services, and website.
Security

We implement appropriate security measures to safeguard your personal information. Your data is encrypted and securely stored.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for shipping, payment processing, and analytics. These parties only access information necessary for their services.

Cookies

We use cookies to personalize content, track performance, and enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. All changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sanriocollections.
